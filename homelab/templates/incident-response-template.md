# Incident Response Report

## Overview
Brief summary of the incident.

- Type of incident (malware, unauthorized access, misconfiguration, etc.)
- Date/time (real or simulated)
- Scope of impact

## Detection

- Alert source (SIEM, logs, user report, etc.)
- Indicators observed
- Initial trigger event

---

## Environment

Systems and infrastructure involved.

- Hosts / endpoints
- Network segments (VLANs, subnets)
- Services affected

---

## Initial Assessment

Early understanding of the situation.

- Suspected cause
- Potential impact
- Risk level (low / medium / high)

---

## Investigation

- Logs reviewed
- Commands or tools used
- Timeline of events

- Isolation steps (network, host, account)

## Eradication

How the root cause was removed.

- Malware removal
- Configuration fixes
- Credential resets
- Patch or update actions

---

## Recovery

Steps taken to restore normal operations.

- System restoration
- Validation testing
- Monitoring after recovery

---

## Lessons Learned

- What worked well
- What could be improved
- Gaps identified in detection or response
